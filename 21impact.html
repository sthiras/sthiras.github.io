21impact

Branding
Role: Designer
Client: ShareInvestor Thailand

21impact, a ShareInvestor Thailand subsidiary, is a technology company that combines human-oriented approaches and the best-of-breed technology to deliver innovative digital solutions and provide professional consult, with the slogan “We create digital platforms with maximum impact in the 21st century.”

I developed the corporate identity for 21impact, involving designing the logo, visual guidelines, as well as making the brand book.

Since the company sees itself as a new wave that brings impact to the modern world and is firmly committed to the human-oriented principle, I put together three main keywords, “technology”, “movement”, and “human” as a starting point. I explored multiple directions to translate them into unique design concepts. In the final logo design, I decided on a wordmark inspired by wave shapes and human handwriting, to represent a relationship between humans and technology according to the company values.

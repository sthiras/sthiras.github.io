Finding Abstraction
BACC Exhibition

Branding, Eviromental Graphics
Role: Designer
With: Bangkok Art & Culture Centre

Finding Abstraction, by BACC, was a Thai abstract sculptural art exhibition featuring works of four Thai renowned artists. The exhibition is a presentation of different creative processes involved, from origin of ideas, study, discovery of attributes found in abstraction, and communication in form of abstraction that is channeled into a sculptural form.

I worked with BACC exhibition department to design a visual identity for the exhibition, responsible for designing visual communication on all platforms, including print, digital, and environmental graphics.

Starting by studying the works and creative processes of the artists, I found the nature of abstract sculptures to be interesting, they are non-objective, indistinct, and sometimes act like a puzzle for the viewer to solve. I decided to take inspiration from that.

I experimented with abstract and simplified shapes, forms, and letters. The final logo consists of shapes that are derived from some of the sculptures, and a typography designed to be a little bit of a puzzle to be solved.

The objective of this exhibition is to show that the creation of abstract art is not something out of reach for the audience. So I incorporate some pop elements into the overall mood and tone, color scheme, and composition to imply approachability.

Vanich

Branding
Role: Designer
With: Coostudio
Client: Vanich Brand

Vanich is a fashion brand by a young Thai designer who brings traditional Thai silk into youthful and fresh designs. Their garments are meticulously tailor made. "For only you shine through every time you wear” is their motto.

I designed the identity for the brand, including logo, visual guidelines, and brand book.

The first thing I noticed from their dresses was the colorfulness and shininess of the silk fabric in classic-inspired silhouettes. Smocking and ruffles are distinctive details usually found in their designs. I wanted to capture the mischievous spirit of the brand and represent it in the visual identity. For the logo, I tried several iterations to make sure they convey flamboyance and playfulness which are the keywords I aimed for. I settled on a modern high-contrast lettering design with letterforms inspired by curves and details from the dresses. The brand’s typefaces are chosen to match the style of the logo, with the heading font being more expressive while the text font is more neutral and easier to read. For the colors, black and white color serves well as main colors since they give a clean and luxurious look and also don’t interfere with colorful garments. Metallic color and holographic effect help add some playful personality.
